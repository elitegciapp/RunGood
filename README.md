# Realistic Running App – Product Spec (v1)

## App Purpose
A beginner-friendly, Apple Store–compliant running app designed for busy people with inconsistent schedules.
The app prioritizes consistency, realism, and burnout prevention over performance metrics.

Core promise:
This app helps busy people run consistently without feeling bad when life gets in the way.

---

## Target User
- Beginners or returning runners
- Walkers transitioning to running
- Deconditioned but mobile
- Busy schedules
- History of inconsistency with fitness apps

---

## Platform
- iOS (primary)
- Apple Watch (optional)
- Apple Health integration

---

## Core Principles
- Time-based workouts only
- No pace or distance pressure
- Run/walk by default
- User autonomy over plan adjustments
- Gentle progression
- Burnout prevention is enforced automatically

---

## Workout Styles
User-selectable and changeable:
- Walk → Jog progression
- Run/Walk intervals
- Easy continuous running
- Bare-minimum sessions (short duration)

---

## Plan Structure
- Default: 2–3 runs per week
- Session length: 10–30 minutes
- Weekly progression capped
- Deload weeks inserted automatically

---

## Missed Workout Logic
When workouts are skipped:
User is prompted to choose:
- Reduce intensity slightly
- Skip and continue
- Reset the current week

The app does not shame or penalize missed workouts.

---

## Notifications
- Motivational nudges only
- No guilt-based language
- Fully optional

---

## Health Integrations
### Apple Health
Reads:
- Steps
- Active minutes
- Workout history

Writes:
- Completed running workouts
- Duration

### Apple Watch
- Optional support
- Time-based interval cues
- Simple workout display
- Haptic feedback for transitions

---

## Monetization
- Free trial
- Subscription required for:
  - Ongoing plan adaptation
  - Apple Watch support
  - Advanced burnout protection
  - Historical insights

---

## MVP Scope
Included:
- Onboarding questionnaire
- Workout style selection
- Plan generation
- Missed workout handling
- HealthKit sync
- Subscription flow

Excluded (v1):
- Social features
- Coaching chat
- Race-specific training
- Nutrition tracking

---

## Tone & Copy
- Dry realism
- Encouraging
- Honest
- No hype
- No guilt language

---

## Success Metrics
- Weekly active users
- Run completion rate
- Consistency streaks (soft)
- Churn after free trial
